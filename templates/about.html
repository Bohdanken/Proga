Welcome to our site!

We provide real-time alarm predictions for regions using advanced machine learning models. Our system uses a combination of weather and lemmatized text data from the Institute for the Study of War (ISW) to make 24-hour alarm predictions for specific regions.

Our machine learning models are trained to analyze various weather and text data parameters, such as wind speed, humidity, temperature, and other relevant factors that could influence the likelihood of an alarm. By analyzing this data, our models can accurately predict the likelihood of an alarm occurring within the next 24 hours.

The site is running on a Random Forest Classifier model, which is trained on data through January 20 2023. In the future, we plan to retrain the models using the latest information.

Also under development is the ability to give the user the function of selecting a model for the predictions, with their description and accuracy.
